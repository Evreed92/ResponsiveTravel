Granada, Spain

Filled with history and culture, Granada Spain will take your breath away. From the towering heights of the ancient fortress of Alhambra, to the beautiful mountain views, Granada has everything you need for a one of a kind Vacation Destination.

Attractions

There a variety of things to do in Granada:

  • Take a tour through Alhambra
  • See a Flamenco Show
  • Safari through the Sierra Nevadas
  • Cordoba and its Mosque
